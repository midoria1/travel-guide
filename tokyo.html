Tokyo

Akihabara, located in central Tokyo, is world-famous as the heart of anime, manga, and gaming culture. It's a paradise for otakus, packed with multi-story stores like Animate, Mandarake, and Gamers Akihabara, offering everything from manga and Blu-rays to figures and exclusive merchandise. Super Potato is a must-visit for retro game lovers, while Radio Kaikan is filled with collectibles and model kits. The district is also home to unique experiences like maid cafés (such as @Home Café and Maidreamin), where waitresses dressed as anime characters serve food with performances and games. For idol fans, Don Quijote Akihabara houses the AKB48 Theater, and the area is full of Gachapon Halls with hundreds of capsule toy machines. With places like Akihabara UDX hosting anime events and cosplay gatherings, Akihabara is a dream destination for anyone passionate about Japanese pop culture
