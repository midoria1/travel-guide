Paris

Paris is the capital city of France and one of the most famous cities in the world. Known as the "City of Light," Paris is celebrated for its rich history, stunning architecture, and romantic atmosphere. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The city is also a global center for art, fashion, and cuisine, attracting millions of tourists every year. Whether you're walking along the Seine River or enjoying a croissant in a charming café, Paris offers an unforgettable experience
